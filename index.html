About The Visualization

The dashboard is a visualization of bacterial data found in the belly buttons volunteers. There are 3 charts that show a diferent set of data; The bar chart shows the 10 most common bacterial species, the bubble chart shows the species with the highest concentration per individual, and the gauge chart shows the washing frequency for each individual.
